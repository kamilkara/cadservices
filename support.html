🛠️ Help & Documentation

The "Curly Bracket" Rule

If you want to trigger a function key like Rename, you must type {F2} in the editor. Typing just F2 will send the letters "F" and "2" sequentially.

Admin Privileges

If your CAD software (like Revit or AutoCAD) is "Running as Administrator," OmniHUD needs the same level of permission. Right-click OmniHUD.exe and select "Run as Administrator."

Sync & Run

Every time you change a macro or hardware mapping, hit the Sync & Run button. This re-compiles your settings and refreshes the background engine.

🐛 Known Issues

I believe in transparency. Here is what I am currently fixing:

  • Multi-Monitor Drift: If you swap primary monitors while the app is running, the HUD might drift. Simply drag it back. A fix for auto-alignment is coming in V1.1.
  • Antivirus False-Positives: Since OmniHUD is an "unsigned" indie app that sends keystrokes, Windows Defender might flag it. This is a false-positive.
  • Revit Focus Lag: Occasionally, switching between large projects might take a second for the HUD to update.

🔐 Privacy Policy

Minimum Data Collection

I only collect what is strictly necessary: your email (from Gumroad) and your Hardware ID (HWID) to manage your 2-device activation limit.

No Keylogging

OmniHUD is a macro-sender, not a recorder. I do not log your keystrokes, I do not watch your files, and I definitely don't sell your data.

Email Usage

I hate spam. I will only email you for critical technical updates or new "Recipe" shares. You can opt-out at any time.
